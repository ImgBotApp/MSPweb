People

The group was set up on 1st July 2008 within the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at the University of Strathclyde and comprises

Prof. Neil Ghani
who is currently working on theories of advanced data types, containers, induction recursion, parametricity and all areas of category theory. See Neil's current homepage for more details or email Neil.Ghani [at] cis.strath.ac.uk. And, here is a picture of Neil.
Dr Conor McBride
who is currently working on dependently typed programming and, in particular, Epigram. He is also interested in extending the functionality of Haskell so that it can mimic the dependently-typed style of programming. See Conor's current homepaqe for more details or email Conor.McBride [at] cis.strath.ac.uk.
Dr Clemens Kupke
who is currently working on both databases and coalgebras. See Clemen's homepaqe for more details or email Clemens.Kupke [at] cis.strath.ac.uk.
Adam Gundry
who is a PhD student under Conor McBride's direction on the topic of adding Nat-indexed families to Haskell.
Pierre-Evariste Dagand
who is a PhD student under Conor McBride's direction on the topic of reusability and dependent types.
Lorenzo Malatesta
who is a PhD student under Neil Ghani's direction on the topic of induction-recursion.
Guillaume Allais
who is a PhD student under Conor McBride's direction. Guillaume's webpage.
Stevan Andjelkovic
who is a PhD student under Conor McBride's direction.
Federico Orsanigo
who is a PhD student under Patricia Johann's direction.
Tim Revell
who is a PhD student under Neil Ghani's direction.

Alumni

Dr Peter G. Hancock
who is interested in logic, type theory and computer systems. He worked with Prof. Neil Ghani on induction recursion. See Peter's current homepage for more details or email hancock [at] spamcop.net.
Clement Fumex
was a PhD student under Patricia Johann's direction on the topic of generic induction principles. Clement completed his PhD in 2012. Clement's thesis is entitled "Induction and Coinduction schemes in Category Theory". His examiners were Bart Jacobs (external) and Conor McBride (internal).
Dr Patricia Johann
who worked on advanced data types and categorical and operational models of parametricity. Patricia left in 2013 to take up a Chair at Appalachian State University.
Dr Robert Atkey
who worked as an RA on Prof Ghani and Dr Johann's grant Categorical Foundations of indexed programming. Bob left in 2013.
Dr Sam Lindley
who worked as an RA on Dr McBride's grant Haskell Types with Added Value.
